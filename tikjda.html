TIKJDA

Description

Escape to the breathtaking mountain resort of Tikjda, nestled in the heart of the Djurdjura National Park. Enjoy the stunning views, fresh mountain air, and a day filled with adventure.

Activities

  • Guided hike through scenic trails.
  • Snow activities (winter season) or nature exploration (summer season).
  • Picnic in the picturesque surroundings.

Prices

  • Adults: 3,500 DA
  • Children (under 12): 2,000 DA

(Includes transportation, guide, and picnic lunch.)
